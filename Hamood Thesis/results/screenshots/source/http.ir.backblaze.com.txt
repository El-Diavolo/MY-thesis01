<h2>Corporate Profile</h2>
<div class="field field--name-field-nir-global-block-node field--type-entity-reference field--label-hidden field__item">
<div class="field field--name-field-nir-global-block-body field--type-text-long field--label-hidden field__item"><p style="padding:25px 0 50px; text-align:center; max-width:70%; margin:0 auto">The leading independent storage cloud—serving 500K+ customers in 175+ countries, including businesses, developers, IT professionals, and individuals. Our proprietary software layer and 2+ exabytes of data storage deliver two services:</p>
<div class="row">
<div class="col-md-6"><img alt="News Releases Icon" class="card-img align-center" src="sites/g/files/knoqqb76836/themes/site/nir_pid4177/dist/images/bb-icon-b2cloudstorage.svg"><h5 style="padding:15px;text-align:center;">B2 Cloud Storage</h5>
<p style="padding:0 25px 25px 25px;text-align:center;">A Storage-as-a-Service (STaaS) disruptor, this “pay-as-you-go” offering supports app development, ransomware protection, backup, multi-cloud, and many other use cases. B2 Cloud Storage’s ease of use and affordability differentiate it in a market projected by IDC to reach $91 billion in 2025.</p>
</div>
<div class="col-md-6"><img alt="Featured Items Icon" class="card-img align-center" src="sites/g/files/knoqqb76836/themes/site/nir_pid4177/dist/images/bb-icon-computerbackup.svg"><h5 style="padding:15px;text-align:center;">Computer Backup</h5>
<p style="padding:0 25px 25px 25px;text-align:center;">Automatic, unlimited backup for laptops and desktops. This subscription-based service protects businesses and individuals from ransomware, theft, and data loss, while providing remote access and more. Ranked “best cloud backup service for most people” by The New York Times’ Wirecutter (August 2021).</p>
